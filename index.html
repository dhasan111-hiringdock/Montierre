The House

The House of Montierre

Montierre is a modern perfume house rooted in royal heritage and nocturnal elegance. Each composition is treated as a portrait of a moment in time, captured through scent rather than image. The focus is on atmosphere and presence, not ornament.

The Montierre portfolio is presented as a curated exhibition, where every fragrance is an individual work and every collection a chapter in an unfolding story.

Collections

The Montierre Portfolio

Three collections, fifteen fragrances. Each collection traces a different facet of quiet power and refined presence, from candlelit courts to midnight streets and whispered confessions.

Cour Royale

Inspired by the silent authority of royal courts, Cour Royale captures the balance of strength, grace, and mystery. These fragrances are composed for presence rather than attention.

Noir Silhouette

A study of the city after dark. Noir Silhouette traces the sharp outlines of glass towers, wet pavement, and distant neon, translating modern architecture into scent.

Eternal Veil

Soft, luminous compositions that linger like a whispered secret. Eternal Veil explores intimate radiance, close to the skin and endlessly unfolding.

Cedar Flame

A rich woody-amber composition of sage, tobacco, patchouli, and creamy woods — intense, luxurious, and commanding. The opening is aromatic and warm, carrying hints of sage and spice that feel immediately bold. As the fragrance evolves, tobacco and woods emerge, wrapped in creamy amber and resinous notes that burn slowly and leave a powerful impression.

Cedar Storm

Noir Silhouette

Noir Silhouette traces the city after dark. Glass towers, quiet corridors, the glow of distant neon reflected on wet stone these fragrances frame the outline of a life lived in composed shadows. Each scent is a contour rather than a spotlight, designed for those who prefer definition over display.

A deep, indulgent amber-floral gourmand layered with orchid, dark chocolate, spices, and creamy woods — bold, sensual, and dramatic. Rich and enveloping from the first breath, dark fruits and florals unfold with a mysterious warmth. Spices and orchid deepen the experience, melting into chocolate-toned woods and amber, leaving behind a trail that feels bold, sensual, and unforgettable.

Collection III

Eternal Veil

Eternal Veil explores luminous softness. These fragrances sit close to the skin, unfolding with patience and restraint like light filtered through sheer fabric or a memory returning in fragments. Designed not to project, but to surround, they are signatures meant for the wearer before anyone else.

Citrus Drift

A vibrant citrus-aromatic fragrance layered with mint, lavender, and smooth amber-vanilla — refreshing, clean, and effortlessly modern. Fresh green notes and citrus peel rise together, bright and refreshing from the first moment. As it settles, soft florals and warm undertones emerge, creating a smooth balance that feels clean, uplifting, and perfectly relaxed.

Top

Mugwort, Mint Leaf, Orange Peel

Heart

Benzoin, Silver Lavender, Orange Blossom

Base

Tonka Bean, Amberwood, Vanilla

Sugar Veil

A warm, sweet, intimate gourmand-amber-floral fragrance built around vanilla orchid, brown sugar, tonka bean, and amber-rich woods — soft, modern, irresistible. Soft florals drift in gently, creamy and inviting, before giving way to a warm sweetness that feels comforting rather than loud. As it settles, ambered woods and musk create a smooth, intimate aura that stays close and lingers beautifully.

Base
